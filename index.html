About BikeDoc


The BikeDoc's mission is to provide reliable, 24/7 emergency bike care for cyclists around the city. We are dedicated to making bike repairs accessible and convenient for riders on the go by deploying vending machines stocked with high-quality repair tools. Our goal is to place a BikeDoc along some of the most traveled routes hoping to inspire cyclists to go the extra mile, knowing the BikeDoc will be there when needed.

No more struggling to find a bike shop or carrying bulky tools on a ride. With Bikedoc, cyclists can easily access the tools they need to keep their bikes in top condition, wherever they are.

Be sure to follow us on social media for information on new locations and events!
